Classical Pieces

Salida Del Sol

"Salida del Sol" is a classical guitar composition from the 1900s that I learned in 2021. It remains one of my most accomplished pieces. Set in an andante tempo and structured as A-B-A, this composition showcases a precise blend of melody and harmony. As part of my classical repertoire, "Salida del Sol" offers a serene musical journey, evoking imagery of a slow sunrise over peaceful landscapes through its measured and calculated composition.
