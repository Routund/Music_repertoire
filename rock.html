Tears in Heaven

This is quite a sad song, being made after Eric Claptons son fell out of a window. I learned this in 2023, and can play it in full. The bridge on the song is quite nice to play, being comprised of chords played in a broken chord fashion. The verses arent't difficult, but in one verse playing theres two versions of the verse, and if Im not paying attention I might accidentally play the wrong version, which sounds out of sync and strange when paired with a backing track. Its still a nice osunding verse though, and the riff at the start of each playing othe verse is quite nice to learn.

Nothing else matters

This currently the song I am working on learning. It has a whole lot of techniques, and is therefore quite a challening piece for me as it has everything from bar chords, pulls oofs and hammer ons, harmonics and more. It's not a hard song by any means, just a bit hard to learn. This however does make it a fun song to play, and I quite enjoy playing the parts with copious amounts of hammer ons and pull ofs in the intro. I will update this section with more info on the playing of the song once I've finished learning it as I still havent got into learning the verse and chorus yet, although they should be quite simple as most of the complexity is contained in the intro with the solo guitar.
